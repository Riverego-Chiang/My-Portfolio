Research 01 / Media & Society

The Platformization of Public Discourse & Elite Alliances 公共话语平台化与精英联盟研究

This academic paper explores the intricate relationship between political capital, elite alignment, and polarization on modern social platforms. Applying advanced sociological frameworks, the study unpacks how tactical collaborations appropriate communicative capital to reshape public sentiment. Through strict qualitative analysis, the research demystifies the mechanisms of algorithmic amplification and ideological echo chambers. 本篇学术论文探讨了现代社交媒体平台上政治资本、精英结盟与群体极化之间的复杂关系。研究引入了前沿的社会学理论框架，深刻剖析了策略性合作如何挪用传播资本来重塑公众情绪。通过严格的质化研究方法，本项工作揭示了算法放大机制与意识形态回音室的底层逻辑。
